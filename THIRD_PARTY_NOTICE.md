## Excluded Third-Party Materials

To keep this repository public-shareable and lightweight, local copies of third-party papers, local app binary artifacts, and raw datasets are intentionally excluded from version control.

Excluded content categories include:
- Literature PDFs previously stored in `papers/`
- Binary simulation/app artifacts previously stored in `app/`
- Raw/local datasets under `data/`

## How to Rebuild Local Research Context

- Use references and citations already present in project markdown documents.
- Obtain papers and datasets from their official publishers or dataset providers.
- Keep local copies outside Git or under ignored paths defined in `.gitignore`.
